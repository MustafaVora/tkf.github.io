How it works

  1. Choose a frame: Browse our collection and pick the design you love.
  2. Upload or send a photo: Use the customize button or message us on WhatsApp.
  3. Preview: See a live preview. We offer free crop and colour adjustments on request.
  4. Confirm & pay: Use the simple checkout (email order) — we’ll confirm and start making your frame.
  5. Ship: We pack carefully and ship within 3–5 business days.

FAQs

Yes — message us with dimensions and we’ll quote.

Yes — add a short note when placing the order.
